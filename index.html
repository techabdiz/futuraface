Our Product Offerings

“Our Product suite offers scalable, sustainable and cost-effective solutions that will help our clients realize increase in market share”

Our insurance solutions are designed to be offered both as an integrated solution as well as modular solutions that supports the complete life cycle of the insurance policy administration

Core Policy Administration
  • Ninja is a framework used for modernization of legacy policy admin systems
  • Built on contemporary technologies - Angular, Spring boot, Python & Flutter
  • Friendly user interface with intuitive menu navigation
  • Enables rapid conversion of applications from legacy to modern technologies








Agency Management
  • Agency Management System (AMS) offers comprehensive functionality
  • Designed to support the end to end process
    o Agent on-boarding to Agent termination
  • Multiple hierarchies can be defined
  • Incentive calculations at different hierarchical levels
  • Allows agency transfers, promotions and the related compensation changes
  • Campaign management and targets
  • Accounting entries generated for the financial transactions
  • Capability to integrate with third party systems using API
  • Report generation
Integration Utilities
  • Bulk upload utility – insured members, demography data etc
  • Upload data into policy administration system
    o Premium rates
    o Bank file - Premium collections & reversals
    o Commission rates
    o Charges and expenses
Digital Offering
  • MRTA/Loan Cover insurance policy creation from mobile devices
  • Agency Management System offered in mobile phones
  • Proposal data management in mobile devices
  • Policy enquiries - policy status, surrender values, due dates from mobile device
